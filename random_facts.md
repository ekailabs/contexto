# Obscure World Facts

## Animal Kingdom

*   **Wombat Poop:** Wombats are the only animals in the world known to produce cube-shaped poop. This shape stops it from rolling away and helps mark their territory.
*   **Octopus Anatomy:** An octopus has three hearts. Two pump blood to the gills, while the third pumps it to the rest of the body. When an octopus swims, the third heart stops beating, which is why they prefer to crawl rather than swim.
*   **Flamingo Diet:** Flamingos are not naturally pink. They are born grey, and their color comes from a pigment called canthaxanthin, which is found in their diet of brine shrimp and blue-green algae.

## Geography and Space

*   **Venus Rotation:** Venus spins in the opposite direction of most planets in our solar system. A day on Venus (one full rotation) is actually longer than a year on Venus (one orbit around the sun).
*   **Mount Everest:** Mount Everest grows about 4 millimeters (0.16 inches) every year. This is due to the upward thrust caused by the ongoing collision between the Indian and Eurasian tectonic plates.

## History

*   **Oxford Older than Aztecs:** Oxford University is older than the Aztec Empire. The university opened to students in 1096, whereas the Aztec city of Tenochtitlán was founded in 1325.
*   **Cleopatra's Timeline:** Cleopatra lived closer in time to the building of the first Pizza Hut (1958) than to the building of the Great Pyramid of Giza (c. 2560 BC).

## Culinary Oddities

*   **Banana Classification:** Botanically speaking, a banana is specifically classified as a berry. However, a strawberry is technically not a berry at all.
*   **Honey Never Spoils:** Archaeologists have found pots of honey in ancient Egyptian tombs that are over 3,000 years old and still perfectly edible. It’s the only food that doesn't spoil.
